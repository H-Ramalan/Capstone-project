ABUJA INTERNATIONAL HOUSING SHOW 2023!

Abuja International Housing Show has emerged as the largest housing and construction event showcase in Nigeria. With a proven record of excellence in all 16 editions,
Abuja International Housing Show has been meeting the teeming needs of the built industry and the market it serves in the most dynamic way.
The Abuja International Housing Show features Products Presentation, Exhibition of Building Technology, home interiors, modern construction equipment, chemical and paints products, mortgage services, housing projects, interactive forums with prominent stakeholders in the built industry towards achieving affordable housing from all part of the world.

Abuja International Housing Show 2023 Logo

The logo of the Abuja International Housing Show was designed by Asteria Alliance.
The Abuja International Housing Show Summit logo was unveiled to the public on the 24th of April, 2023 at the International Conference Center, Abuja.
The logo carefully depicts the state of the Abuja built industry and how fast the industry has developed.
